Question 2: What are the benefits of using Meta Tags.

Solution: 
  
     Meta tags offer several benefits:

1. SEO Boost: Improve search engine rankings and visibility.
2. Higher Click-Through Rates: Compelling meta descriptions attract more clicks.
3. Clear Content Communication: Concisely represent webpage content.
4. Positive User Experience: Contribute to a user-friendly interface.
5. Social Media Optimization: Control how content appears when shared.
6. Enhanced Accessibility: Contribute to responsive design and accessibility.
7. Structured Data and Rich Snippets: Help search engines understand content structure.
8. Prevent Duplicate Content Issues: Specify preferred versions of pages.
9. SEO Flexibility: Adapt to changes in content, keywords, and promotional messages.
